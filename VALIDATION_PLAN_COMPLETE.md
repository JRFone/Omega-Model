# Omega FISH Validation Plan

## Level 1 — Unit mathematics

Test survival, movement conservation, growth, maturity, selectivity, Baranov catch, Beverton–Holt recruitment, ageing error and every likelihood against hand calculations.

## Level 2 — Known-truth recovery

Simulate data from known parameters, fit the model and measure bias, precision, interval coverage and failure rates over hundreds of replicates.

## Level 3 — Misspecification

Generate truth with changing catchability, recruitment regimes, movement, ageing bias, catch under-reporting and time-varying selectivity. Fit simpler models and quantify resulting stock-status bias.

## Level 4 — Cross-software benchmark

Reproduce selected Stock Synthesis examples using matched structure and inputs. Compare annual abundance, biomass, recruitment, catches, likelihood components and reference points within declared tolerances.

## Level 5 — Real assessment replication

Rebuild selected published assessments using original inputs and compare results. Investigate every material difference rather than calibrating only the terminal depletion.

## Level 6 — Independent review

Publish code, mathematical documentation, test data and results for independent code and scientific review.
